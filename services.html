Teeth Cleaning

Simple Teeth Cleaning - £5

A generic teeth clean can be done using a brush and doggy toothpaste. This is great for dogs who don't have much buildup of plaque and for owners who want to keep on top of their dog's dental hygiene.

This can be done as a standalone service or an optional extra added onto a groom.

Advanced Teeth Clean - From £20

An advanced teeth clean is performed with an ultrasonic toothbrush designed to target underneath the plaque and help lift it away leaving a nice, clean tooth behind. This method can help reduce inflammation, redness, fight against bacteria, and reduce smelly breath.

This can sometimes take a couple of sessions but is highly recommended for dogs with a buildup on their teeth.

This can be done as a standalone service or an optional extra added onto a groom.
£30 for 1st session. £20 for each following session

Training Sessions - £25

Some dogs don't like being groomed and that's okay! Not everyone enjoys the salon experience and it can be scary for some, especially if they have not had puppy training in a salon environment. We offer training sessions to help show dogs that you don't need to be afraid and it can be an enjoyable experience.

Training sessions can vary from dog to dog. We recommend bringing high-value treats e.g. chicken/cheese/etc. to these appointments as this engages the dogs brain more with giving them their favourite treat. While some dogs only need the odd session, others may need more frequent sessions.

Standard session pricing is £25.
Multiple session pricing can be discussed during a consultation.
